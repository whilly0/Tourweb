Obudu Mountains, Cross River-State

**Obudu Mountain**, located in Cross River State, Nigeria, is a stunning natural destination known for its cool climate, breathtaking views, and lush greenery. Perched on the Obudu Plateau, it features the renowned **Obudu Mountain Resort**, offering luxurious accommodations, a cable car ride with scenic views, and access to hiking trails. Visitors can explore its serene environment, natural pools, waterfalls, and unique wildlife, making it perfect for relaxation and adventure. Its temperate weather, averaging 10°C to 26°C, provides a refreshing escape, particularly during the cooler months of October to February. Obudu Mountain is a must-visit for anyone seeking nature’s tranquility.

At the Obudu Mountain Resort, enjoy luxury accommodations, an exhilarating cable car ride with panoramic views, and scenic hiking trails through lush forests and waterfalls. For adventure seekers, the resort offers exciting activities like mountain biking, bird watching, and exploring natural pools. Whether you’re looking to unwind in a peaceful environment, indulge in local cuisine, or immerse yourself in nature’s wonders, Obudu Mountain is the perfect place for an unforgettable getaway. With its unique climate and beautiful surroundings, it’s a must-visit for anyone seeking both adventure and relaxation
