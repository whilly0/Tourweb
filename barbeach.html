Bar Beach

Bar Beach, situated along the Atlantic Ocean coastline in Victoria Island, Lagos, was one of the most popular beaches in Nigeria. It gained its name from the sandbars that formed a natural barrier, creating a lagoon between the beach and the open ocean.

Despite its transformation and challenges, Bar Beach remains a symbol of Lagos's cultural heritage and coastal identity. While its prominence as a recreational destination may have waned, it continues to hold a place in the memories of those who frequented its shores.
